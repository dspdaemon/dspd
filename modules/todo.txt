MODULES
=======
mod_alsaudev:	 Udev hotplug support for alsa
mod_ossdspioctl: /dev/dsp style ioctls for client stream
mod_ossmixerioctl: /dev/mixer style ioctl
mod_alsaioctl: alsa+sndio pcm ioctls
mod_osscompat: use CUSE to implement oss ioctls
mod_socksrv: socket server.  pass through various commands
	     with remote bit set.
